Core Intelligence Engines

Five Engines. One Complete AI Brand Intelligence Platform.

AI Brand Visibility Intelligence combines answer-layer mapping, factual verification, reputation drift detection, citation intelligence, and prompt-variance stress testing into one unified SaaS platform no-code, real-time, data-driven.

Engine 01

Answer-Layer Brand Representation Mapping (ABRM)

Evaluates narrative placement, comparative framing, and contextual prominence within synthesised AI answers not just tracking mentions but analysing representation.

The ABRM analyses how your brand appears in the context of AI-generated responses: whether you are recommended first, described accurately, and framed favourably compared to competitors. Generates actionable Visibility Scores from every prompt query.
Engine 02

Brand Truth Verification Model (BTVM)

Automatically checks AI-generated claims against verified brand data pricing, features, policies transforming brand governance from passive monitoring to active fact-verification.

The BTVM catches AI hallucinations and outdated brand information before they damage customer trust. If ChatGPT quotes the wrong price or Gemini attributes an incorrect policy, the BTVM flags it immediately with evidence-based remediation recommendations.
Engine 03

AI Reputation Drift Detection Engine (ARDDE)

Detects the gradual divergence between a brand's real-world positioning and the distorted or biased version represented by AI systems over time.

The ARDDE monitors longitudinal shifts in brand perception across all major LLMs, alerting you when AI answers begin diverging from your authentic brand narrative. Tracks drift monthly, quarterly, and annually to provide a full reputation health timeline.

Full Platform

Five Intelligence Engines.
One Brand Command Centre.

Every feature is built specifically for the generative AI era not retrofitted from legacy SEO tools. Our proprietary scoring systems translate complex AI behaviours into actionable commercial KPIs that marketing teams and agencies actually need.

Engine 01 · ABRM

Answer-Layer Brand Representation Mapping

Evaluates how your brand is narratively positioned in AI-generated answers measuring contextual prominence, comparative framing, and citation accuracy across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude.

Unlike basic mention tracking, ABRM analyses the quality of brand representation: are you described accurately? Are you recommended favourably compared to competitors? Does the AI answer reflect your actual value proposition? Every prompt run generates a structured Visibility Score with improvement recommendations.
Engine 02 · BTVM

Brand Truth Verification Model

Automatically checks AI-generated claims against your verified brand data pricing, features, policies, service coverage transforming brand governance from passive monitoring to active fact-verification.

The BTVM is your brand's factual firewall against AI hallucinations. It maintains a structured "Brand Truth" data model that every AI answer is cross-checked against. When inaccuracies are detected, the system identifies the upstream source and provides remediation guidance to correct the AI narrative pipeline.
Engine 03 · ARDDE

AI Reputation Drift Detection Engine

Detects "AI reputation drift" the gradual divergence between a brand's real-world positioning and the distorted or biased version represented by AI systems over time.

Brands evolve. AI models don't always keep up. The ARDDE monitors longitudinal shifts across all connected LLMs flagging when AI answers begin diverging from your brand narrative. Monthly drift reports give marketing teams an early warning system before reputational damage reaches consumers.
Engine 04 · CIGAD

Citation Influence Graph for AI Discovery

Maps the upstream content ecosystem identifying which specific domains, forums, publishers, and knowledge bases disproportionately shape the answers synthesised across different AI models.

The CIGAD answers the critical question: "What content is shaping how AI describes my brand?" By mapping the citation influence graph, brands can identify which third-party sources to collaborate with, which misinformation pipelines to address, and where to invest content strategy for maximum AI answer impact.
Engine 05 · PBVST

Prompt-Variance Brand Visibility Stress Testing

Uses systematic semantic variations to measure how stable your brand's visibility is across different query phrasings creating a resilience profile for generative discovery. Ensures brand representation holds up regardless of how a user phrases their AI question.

The same brand can receive very different AI treatment depending on query phrasing. PBVST runs hundreds of semantic variations of key brand queries to stress-test visibility stability. The output is a Prompt Resilience Score telling you how robust your brand is to query variation and where you are most vulnerable to representation gaps.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI Brand Visibility Intelligence and what it means for your brand in the generative AI era.

What is AI Brand Visibility Intelligence and why do I need it?

+
AI Brand Visibility Intelligence is the practice of monitoring and improving how your brand is represented inside AI-generated answers from platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Claude. As users shift from traditional search engines to AI platforms for discovery and purchase decisions, brands that lack visibility in AI answers are effectively invisible to a growing proportion of their potential customers. AI Brand Visibility provides the infrastructure to measure this visibility, verify factual accuracy, and improve brand representation systematically.

How is this different from traditional SEO tools like SEMrush or Ahrefs?

+
Traditional SEO tools were built for the era of keyword rankings, backlinks, and SERP positions. They cannot analyse how AI systems synthesise information about your brand, detect factual inaccuracies in AI answers, or map the citation sources that influence AI-generated narratives. AI Brand Visibility operates at the answer layer the AI-generated response itself which is an entirely different analytical domain that legacy SEO tools are architecturally incapable of addressing.

Which AI platforms does AI Brand Visibility monitor?

+
The platform currently monitors ChatGPT (OpenAI), Gemini (Google), Perplexity AI, and Claude (Anthropic). The modular architecture is designed to integrate new AI models as they emerge, ensuring the platform remains relevant as the generative AI landscape evolves. Enterprise clients can request custom model integrations based on their specific market exposure.

What sectors does AI Brand Visibility serve?

+
Our platform is purpose-built for UK e-commerce and D2C brands, digital marketing and SEO agencies, B2B SaaS and technology companies, and enterprise brand managers. The platform's sector-specific modules are tailored to the unique AI visibility challenges of each segment from product pricing accuracy for e-commerce to competitive service comparisons for B2B SaaS. High-stakes sectors like healthcare and financial services can access specialist Industry Modules with enhanced factual verification.

Is AI Brand Visibility GDPR compliant?

+
Yes. AI Brand Visibility is architecturally designed to comply with UK GDPR and ICO guidelines. All brand data is processed via secure, anonymised pipelines in UK-based data centres. The platform adheres to ISO/IEC 27001 standards for information security management and implements zero-trust architecture with AES-256 encryption for all brand-specific training data. Full transparency logs are maintained for all AI-generated insights and automated recommendations.

What is "AI reputation drift" and why does it matter?

+
AI reputation drift is the gradual divergence between a brand's real-world positioning and the distorted or biased version represented by AI systems over time. As AI models are trained on historical data, they can perpetuate outdated, inaccurate, or misleading brand information even after a brand has updated its products, pricing, or public positioning. Left unchecked, AI reputation drift directly damages customer trust, conversion rates, and brand equity in the growing proportion of purchase journeys that now involve AI-mediated discovery.

How do I get started with AI Brand Visibility?

+
Getting started is simple no code required. After signing up for your chosen plan, you complete a Brand Truth Model setup (approximately 30 minutes) where you input your verified brand data. The platform then automatically runs your first visibility assessment across all connected AI platforms and delivers your Baseline Report within 24 hours. Onboarding includes a structured walkthrough from our customer success team to ensure you extract maximum value from your first session.
